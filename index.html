Our Chairperson Short Bio

As Chief Commercial Officer at Starhotels Group, I oversee Sales, Marketing, Revenue, and Distribution for 30 properties, leading a team of 85 professionals. Previously, I managed 11 properties as Cluster General Manager in Starhotels' Collezione Division. My career includes roles at Fairmont Hotels & Resorts, MPI, and Starwood Hotels & Resorts, with academic credentials from MPI, Cornell University, HSMAI, and INSEAD's AVIRA course. Actively engaged in professional associations, including leadership roles at the Hotelier Association of Ravenna, Skal International in Monaco, and MPI Italy. Outside work, I enjoy marathon running and live in Florence with my wife Francesca and our children, Alessandro and Leonardo.
